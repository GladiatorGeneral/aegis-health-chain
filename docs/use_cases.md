# Use Cases

## Primary Use Case: Asthma Exacerbation Forecasting

### Problem Statement
Predict 30-day risk of asthma exacerbation using patient clinical data and environmental factors.

### Data Sources
- EHR patient demographics and conditions
- Lab results and vital signs
- Medication history
- Environmental data (pollen, air quality)

### Forecasting Output
- Risk score (0.0-1.0)
- Contributing factors
- Mitigation recommendations
- Confidence intervals

### Stakeholders
- **Patients**: Receive personalized risk alerts
- **Clinicians**: Proactive patient management
- **Researchers**: Model validation and improvement
